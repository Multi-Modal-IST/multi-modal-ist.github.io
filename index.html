MMIST

Welcome to the Multi-modal IST (MMIST) Project

The project is being developed by the Institute for Systems and Robotics (ISR-Lisboa).

Our goal is to acquire, curate, and publicly release multi-modal datasets of different types of cancer to support and advance the research on personalized medicine for oncology patients.

Our compromises are:

    1.Curate and release new datasets;

    2.Periodically update and improve the existing datasets;

    3.Provide benchmarks for different tasks using our datasets.
